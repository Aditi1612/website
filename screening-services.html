Chiral Screening Services

Let our experts identify the ideal column and mobile phase conditions for your enantiomeric separation challenge.

Screening Service

Find Your Optimal Separation - Faster

Identifying the right chiral stationary phase for a new compound can be time-consuming and expensive if done by trial and error. Our professional screening service systematically evaluates a comprehensive panel of CellaChrom columns under multiple mobile phase conditions to find the best separation for your specific racemate.

Submit your compound sample and receive a detailed chromatographic report with resolution, selectivity, and recommended starting conditions, saving you weeks of in-house method development time.

Request Screening Service

Screening Panel

CellaChrom® AD-H - Amylose-based, normal phase & reversed phase conditions

CelluloSil® OD-H - Cellulose-based, complementary selectivity

CrownSil® (+/-) - Crown acid CSP for nitrogen-containing compounds

Multiple mobile phases - Hexane/IPA, hexane/EtOH, MeOH/water, ACN/water

SFC conditions available upon request for applicable compounds

How the Screening Service Works

1

Submit Sample

Send us your racemate sample (5-10 mg recommended) with compound information and any known solubility data.

2

Screening

Our scientists run your compound on our standard multi-column panel under predefined conditions (typically 5-10 business days).

3

Analysis

We analyze results for resolution (Rs), selectivity (慣), and peak symmetry across all tested conditions.

4

Report Delivery

You receive a full chromatographic report with overlay plots, recommended conditions, and next-step guidance.
