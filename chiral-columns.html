Chiral HPLC Columns

Our proprietary chiral stationary phases deliver superior enantioselectivity across a broad range of compound classes.

CellaChrom®

Amylose-based polysaccharide chiral stationary phase - 3rd generation (2022). Compatible with CHIRALCEL AD conditions.

Polysaccharide CSP
Chiral Selector: Amylose tris-(3,5-dimethylphenylcarbamate)

Effective for separating racemates with halide, ether, ester, ketone, sulfoxide, alcohol, amine, acid, amide, and aminoalcohol functional groups. Compatible with standard CHIRALCEL AD-H conditions and mobile phases.

CrownSil®

Advanced crown acid-based chiral stationary phase with unique methyl functional group (2023). Exclusive to CellaChrom.

Crown Acid CSP
Chiral Selector: 18-Crown-6 acid with methyl functional group (exclusive CellaChrom technology)

Specifically designed for separating racemates containing nitrogen functionality, including primary amines, amides, and aminoalcohols. The unique methyl group modification provides enhanced selectivity not available in conventional crown-ether columns. Operates in acidic aqueous mobile phases.

CelluloSil®

Cellulose-based polysaccharide chiral stationary phase (2024). Complementary selectivity to amylose-based columns.

Cellulose CSP
Chiral Selector: Cellulose tris-(3,5-dimethylphenylcarbamate)

Complementary enantioselectivity to amylose-based columns. Recommended for compounds that do not separate well on CellaChrom® AD columns. Often used as the second column in a two-column screening strategy.
