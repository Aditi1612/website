About Cellachrom India

The official CellaChrom representative in India — bringing Korean innovation to Indian scientists.

Who We Are

Cellachrom India

Cellachrom India is the official representative of Cellachrom Korea in India, the global chromatography brand of Cellion Biomed Inc., South Korea. We bring the world-class chromatography solutions trusted by researchers, pharmaceutical companies, and analytical laboratories worldwide from Cellachrom™.

As part of the Cellachrom global network, our mission is to make advanced high quality chiral and achiral HPLC columns, preparative columns, guard columns, and specialty chromatography reagents easily accessible to scientists and industries across India in affordable cost.

By combining Korean innovation with a strong Indian service presence, Cellachrom India ensures that cutting-edge chromatography technology is now within your reach.

Our History

25 Years of Chromatography Innovation

A legacy of continuous development in separation science — from Korea to India.

2000

RStech Corporation founded

Dr. SJ. Kim founded RStech Corporation, establishing the foundation for Korean HPLC column manufacturing excellence.

2001

OptimaPak — 1st Generation

First commercialization — OptimaPak (1st gen) established the brand in the chromatography market.

2002

ChiroSil

Crown-ether based chiral column — first proprietary chiral stationary phase technology.

2008

Hector — 2nd Generation

Second generation brand platform with enhanced performance and broader application range.

2018

Cellionbiomed Inc. founded

Dr. SJ. Kim founded Cellionbiomed Inc. to advance the next generation of chromatography solutions.

2022

CellaChrom® — 3rd Generation

Launch of CellaChrom®, the flagship brand offering polysaccharide-based chiral columns with superior enantioselectivity.

2023

CrownSil®

Advanced Crown-ether based chiral column with unique methyl functional group — exclusive to CellaChrom.

2024

CelluloSil® & AmyloSil®

CelluloSil® (Cellulose-based) and AmyloSil® (Amylose-based) — expanding the polysaccharide CSP portfolio. India operations launched.
