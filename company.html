Who We Are

CellaChrom??by CellionBioMed Inc.

CellaChrom??is the flagship HPLC column brand of CellionBioMed Inc., a leading Korean biotechnology and chromatography company headquartered in Daejeon ??Korea's premier science and technology hub.

Founded with a mission to achieve independence in cutting-edge HPLC column manufacture, we have developed multiple generations of chromatographic technology over more than two decades, serving pharmaceutical, biomedical, chemical, and food analysis laboratories worldwide.

Our proprietary technologies include polysaccharide-based chiral stationary phases (CSPs), advanced crown acid-based CSPs, and high-performance achiral reversed-phase columns. The exclusive 18-crown-6 acid CSP with methyl functional group ??available only from CellaChrom ??offers unmatched selectivity for nitrogen-containing compounds including amines, amides, and aminoalcohols.

We are committed to continuously pushing the boundaries of separation science, delivering solutions that help our customers achieve faster, cleaner, and more reliable analytical results.
