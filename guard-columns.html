Guard Columns

Protect your analytical columns and extend their lifetime with our matched guard column systems.

Protection & Performance

Guard Columns for Every CellaChrom Line

Guard columns are an essential investment for any HPLC system. By trapping particulates, strongly retained matrix components, and column-damaging contaminants before they reach your analytical column, guard columns preserve peak shape, extend column lifetime, and protect your data quality.

CellaChrom guard columns are packed with the same stationary phase as our analytical columns, ensuring no change in selectivity or retention when used together. They are available in a cartridge-style format for quick and easy replacement without disturbing the analytical column connection.
