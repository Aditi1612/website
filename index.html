Who We Are

Cellachrom India

Cellachrom India is the official representative of Cellachrom Korea in India, the global chromatography brand of Cellion Biomed Inc., South Korea. We bring the world-class chromatography solutions trusted by researchers, pharmaceutical companies, and analytical laboratories worldwide from Cellachrom™.

As part of the Cellachrom global network, our mission is to make advanced high quality chiral and achiral HPLC columns, preparative columns, guard columns, and specialty chromatography reagents easily accessible to scientists and industries across India in affordable cost.

By combining Korean innovation with a strong Indian service presence, Cellachrom India ensures that cutting-edge chromatography technology is now within your reach.
