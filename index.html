Our Products

HPLC Columns for Every Application

Our column portfolio covers the full range of chiral and achiral separation requirements — from polysaccharide-based chiral columns to guard and general-purpose columns.

CellaChrom®

Amylose-based polysaccharide CSP — 3rd generation (2022). Compatible with CHIRALCEL AD conditions. Broad enantiomeric selectivity.

  • Amylose tris-(3,5-dimethylphenylcarbamate)
  • Normal phase & reversed phase
  • 5 µm and 3 µm particle sizes
View Specs →

CrownSil®

Exclusive 18-crown-6 acid CSP with methyl functional group (2023). Unique selectivity for nitrogen-containing compounds — only from CellaChrom.

  • Primary amines, amides, aminoalcohols
  • Reversed phase (acidic mobile phase)
  • (+) and (−) configurations available
View Specs →

CelluloSil®

Cellulose-based polysaccharide CSP (2024). Complementary selectivity to amylose columns — ideal second column in screening strategies.

  • Cellulose tris-(3,5-dimethylphenylcarbamate)
  • Normal phase & reversed phase
  • SFC-optimized 3 µm available
View Specs →

Hector C18

High-performance reversed-phase C18 column with superior peak shape and broad pH stability. The workhorse for routine and demanding RP-HPLC.

  • Broad pH range 2–8
  • 5 µm and 3 µm particle sizes
  • 4.6 mm and 2.1 mm ID (LC-MS)
View Specs →
C8

Hector C8

Medium-polarity reversed-phase column for polar analytes that are over-retained on C18. Ideal when shorter retention time is preferred.

  • Suitable for polar analytes
  • Consistent batch-to-batch reproducibility
  • High-purity silica base
View Specs →

Chiral Guard Columns

Matched guard columns for CellaChrom®, CrownSil®, and CelluloSil® analytical columns. Same stationary phase — zero impact on selectivity.

  • Cartridge-style easy replacement
  • 4.0 × 10 mm standard dimensions
  • Available for all chiral lines

Achiral Guard Columns

Guard columns for Hector C18 and C8 analytical columns. Protect your investment and maintain peak shape across thousands of injections.

  • Matched C18 and C8 phases
  • Quick-change cartridge system
  • Extend analytical column lifetime

Whelk-O®
