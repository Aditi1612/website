Our Products

HPLC Columns for Every Application

Our column portfolio covers the full range of chiral and achiral separation requirements — from polysaccharide-based chiral columns to guard and general-purpose columns.

Chiral Columns

Polysaccharide-based and crown acid-based chiral stationary phases for enantiomeric separation. Includes CellaChrom®, CrownSil®, and CelluloSil® lines.

  • CellaChrom® — amylose-based (compatible with CHIRALCEL AD)
  • CrownSil® — advanced 18-crown-6 acid CSP
  • CelluloSil® — cellulose-based chiral columns

General (Achiral) Columns

High-performance reversed-phase and normal-phase HPLC columns for routine and challenging achiral separations across a wide range of applications.

  • C18, C8, phenyl, and other bonded phases
  • High-purity silica base material
  • Available in multiple particle sizes

Guard Columns

Protect your analytical columns from sample matrix interferences. Our guard columns extend analytical column lifetime and maintain peak performance.

  • Compatible guard column options for all main columns
  • Easy cartridge replacement system
  • Maintains chromatographic integrity

Why CellaChrom

Korea's Leading HPLC Column Manufacturer

CellaChrom™ is a product of CellionBioMed Inc., headquartered in Daejeon, Korea's science & technology hub. We combine academic research excellence with industrial manufacturing quality to produce chromatography columns that meet the demands of modern pharmaceutical, biomedical, and chemical analysis.

Our proprietary crown acid-based CSP — featuring a unique methyl functional group — is available exclusively from CellaChrom, offering unmatched selectivity for nitrogen-containing compounds such as amines, amides, and aminoalcohols.
