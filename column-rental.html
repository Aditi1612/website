Column Rental Program

Evaluate CellaChrom columns on your own compounds before committing to a purchase.

Try Before You Buy

Flexible Column Evaluation

Choosing the right column is a significant investment. Our column rental program lets you rigorously evaluate CellaChrom columns in your own laboratory, on your own compounds, under your own conditions - with minimal upfront commitment.

Whether you are screening multiple columns for a new project or validating a specific column before a bulk order, our rental program provides a cost-effective and risk-free path to finding your optimal solution.

1

Request & Select

Contact us to discuss your application. Our team will recommend the most suitable columns for evaluation based on your compound class and separation requirements.

2

Evaluate In-House

We ship the rental columns directly to your laboratory. Test them on your HPLC or SFC system with your real sample compounds for the agreed rental period.

3

Buy or Return

If the column meets your needs, convert to a purchase - rental fees may be applied toward the purchase price. If not, simply return the column at the end of the rental period.

Start Your Evaluation

Contact us to inquire about available columns, rental terms, pricing, and shipping arrangements. We serve customers globally.
